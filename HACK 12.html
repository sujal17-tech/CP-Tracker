About

Welcome to our website!

At COMPETITIVE PROGRAMMING TRACKER (CPT), we aim to revolutionize Competitive Programming (CP) tracking within colleges. Currently, there is no centralized system to monitor students' CP performance, making it difficult to track progress, compare rankings, and foster motivation.

Our platform aggregates data from multiple CP platforms like Codeforces, LeetCode, and CodeChef, providing real-time performance analytics, leaderboards, and personalized insights. By offering structured progress tracking and competitive rankings, we empower students to improve their problem-solving skills, stay motivated, and excel in coding competitions.
